Chembra Peak in Wayanad, Kerala, attracts tourists with its stunning natural beauty and adventurous trekking opportunities. Standing at approximately 2,100 meters, it is the highest peak in the region, offering panoramic views of the Western Ghats. The trek to the peak is popular for its picturesque landscapes, lush greenery, and a unique heart-shaped lake that adds a romantic touch to the journey.
